Photographs

Please feel free to send photographs, but keep combined attachments to less than 5MB per message. Please don't crop the pictures - we need to see the edges.

Valuations

We regret we cannot offer valuations by email. We cannot value large collections of books, through we are not interested in buying them.

Response time

Due to high volume of emails we receive each day, we cannot always reply to every offer. If you have not heard from us within 7 days, you can assume we are not interested in purchasing your book. We will always respond by email.
